## Section 12 — What NOT to do (skip list)

Refuse the following during the 30-day rollout. Saying "no" 10 times saves thousands of dollars and zero progress.

1. **Yellow Pages Silver / Gold / Platinum** — high-pressure phone sales, no measurable ROI for B2B trades. Free listing only.
2. **BBB Accreditation ($500–$700/yr)** — defer until 12+ months of operations and 5+ Google reviews exist.
3. **Yelp Ads** — sales call lands within 48 hours. Decline firmly; do not agree to a follow-up.
4. **HomeStars "Verified Pro" badge ($79–$199/mo)** — wait until 10+ organic reviews exist.
5. **Houzz Pro+ ($65–$399/mo)** — Basic delivers the citation value.
6. **Clutch Sponsored placements ($1k+/mo)** — only justifiable for $50k+ average tickets.
7. **"List you on 500 directories for $99" aggregators** (cold-email Brightlocal resellers) — link farms that feed mismatched NAP downstream and hurt the citation graph.
8. **Pay-per-lead platforms** (BuildZoom Pro, Angi/HomeAdvisor leads, Networx) — lead economics never work for commercial FRP. Free profiles only.
9. **Any directory that won't show a sample listing before sign-up** — almost always a scam or content farm.
10. **Changing the phone number, business name, or website URL mid-campaign** — wrecks NAP consistency and resets the citation clock. If a change is unavoidable, propagate it everywhere within 7 days using the Day 30 tracker.
